About us

Technical expertise.
Accountability for documentation.

Quattrominds sp. z o.o. is a design practice specialising in complete building services documentation for commercial, service and healthcare facilities.

We deliver projects across the full phase range — from concept, through Building Permit Design (BD), to Technical and Executive Design (TD+ED) — and provide author supervision during construction.

We work in accordance with Polish building standards and PIIB requirements. Documentation is coordinated across disciplines in-house — the investor receives one complete package.
